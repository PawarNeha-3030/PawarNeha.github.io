Leaf Spot Disease
92% Confidence

Bacterial leaf spot affects many crops including tomatoes, peppers, and cabbage. It appears as small, dark, water-soaked spots on leaves that may enlarge and turn brown.

Recommended Treatment

  • Remove and destroy infected leaves immediately
  • Apply neem oil spray every 7 days
  • Use copper-based fungicide as preventive measure
  • Improve air circulation around plants
  • Avoid overhead watering to prevent spread

Prevention Tips

• Rotate crops annually • Use disease-resistant varieties • Maintain proper spacing between plants • Keep tools clean • Monitor plants regularly for early signs

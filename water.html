Water Conservation Tips

Drip Irrigation

Use drip irrigation systems to deliver water directly to plant roots, reducing evaporation and saving up to 50% water.

Rainwater Harvesting

Collect and store rainwater during monsoon season for use during dry periods. A 1000 sq ft roof can collect ~600 liters per 1cm rain.

Timing Matters

Water plants early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation. Avoid watering during windy conditions.

Mulching

Apply organic mulch around plants to reduce soil moisture evaporation by 25-50% and suppress weeds.

Water Recycling

Reuse household grey water (from washing vegetables, etc.) for watering plants after proper treatment.

Drought-Resistant Crops

Choose crops that require less water like millets, pulses, and certain vegetables suitable for your region.
